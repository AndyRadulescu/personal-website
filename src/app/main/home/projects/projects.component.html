DocumpilerWeb - master thesis

Everybody is tired of completing paperwork! This app comes as a solution for this. Any document can be easily completed by uploading a photo with the ID where the information can be taken from with OCR. The template document will be completed with the info taken from the image. The newly generated document can be downloaded or sent as an email.

All the scanned documents will be available in the app for later use.
